Meerkat

The meerkat (Suricata suricatta) or suricate is a small mongoose discovered in southern Africa. It is characterised by its large head, giant eyes, pointed snout, lengthy legs, skinny tapering tail, and a brindled coat pattern. Meerkats have foreclaws tailored for digging and have the capability to thermoregulate to live on in their harsh, dry habitat.
